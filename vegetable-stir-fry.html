Vegetable Stir-Fry

A quick and healthy stir-fried vegetable dish with a savory sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 cup broccoli florets
  • 1 cup snap peas
  • 1 medium carrot, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 zucchini, sliced
  • 2 green onions, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, grated
  • Salt and pepper to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1/2 tbsp cornstarch (optional, for thickening)
  • 1/4 cup water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the Sauce: In a bowl, whisk soy sauce, sesame oil, rice vinegar, brown sugar, and cornstarch (if using). Add water and set aside.
  2. Cook the Vegetables: In a skillet or wok, heat oil over medium-high. Add garlic and ginger, stirring until fragrant.
  3. Stir-Fry: Add bell pepper, broccoli, snap peas, carrot, mushrooms, zucchini, and green onions. Stir-fry for 5-7 minutes until tender-crisp.
  4. Add the Sauce: Pour sauce into skillet, stir to coat veggies, and cook for 2 more minutes.
  5. Season and Serve: Add salt and pepper to taste. Serve over rice or noodles.
