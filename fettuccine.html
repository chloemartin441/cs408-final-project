Fettuccine Alfredo

A rich and creamy classic Italian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb (450g) fettuccine pasta
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup (115g) unsalted butter
  • 1 cup (100g) freshly grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional, for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Cook the Pasta: In a large pot of salted boiling water, cook the fettuccine according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. Make the Sauce: In a large skillet over medium heat, melt the butter and add the heavy cream. Stir until smooth.
  3. Add the Cheese: Gradually whisk in the grated Parmesan, stirring until the sauce is creamy.
  4. Combine: Add the cooked fettuccine to the skillet and toss to coat.
  5. Season and Serve: Season with salt and pepper, garnish with parsley if desired, and serve immediately.
