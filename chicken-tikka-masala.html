Chicken Tikka Masala

A popular Indian dish with tender chicken in a creamy, spiced sauce.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade:

  • 1 lb (450g) boneless chicken breasts, cubed
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) plain yogurt
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • Salt to taste

For the Sauce:

  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 cup (120ml) tomato puree
  • 1 cup (240ml) heavy cream
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions

  1. Marinate the Chicken: In a bowl, mix yogurt, lemon juice, ginger-garlic paste, cumin, coriander, garam masala, turmeric, chili powder, and salt. Add the chicken and coat well. Cover and marinate for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the Chicken: In a skillet over medium heat, cook the chicken until browned and fully cooked. Set aside.
  3. Make the Sauce: In the same skillet, heat oil or ghee. Add onions and cook until golden. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook until fragrant.
  4. Add Spices and Tomato Puree: Stir in cumin, coriander, paprika, turmeric, and chili powder. Add tomato puree and cook until oil separates.
  5. Finish the Sauce: Add heavy cream, stir, and simmer until thickened. Season with salt.
  6. Combine and Serve: Return chicken to the skillet and stir. Garnish with cilantro and serve with rice or naan.
